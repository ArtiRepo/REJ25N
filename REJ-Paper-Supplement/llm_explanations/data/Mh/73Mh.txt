**Original LTL formula:**
F a

**Rebuild meaning:**
- The operator **F** ("eventually") requires that **a** holds in *at least one future state* of the execution.
- The formula does **not** constrain **a** in the current state or any state before **a** becomes true.
- If **a** never becomes true in any future state, the formula is violated.
