```
    G   // Root (temporal operator)
```

#### Step 3: Rebuild meaning from the bottom of the tree up  
1. **Leaf (`a`):**  
   - Represents a specific condition that can be `true` or `false` at a given time.  
   - *Example meaning:* "The system property `a` holds now." (e.g., "The lights are on").

- **Violation condition:** If `a` is `false` at **any point** in the future (even once), the formula is violated.
